How WQI is calculated (WHO standard method):
1. Each parameter gets a sub-index Qᵢ = ((V – Videal) / (Vlimit – Videal)) × 100
2. WQI = Σ(Qᵢ × Wᵢ)  ·  Weights: pH 25% · Turbidity 25% · Conductivity 20% · D.O. 20% · Temp 10%
Reference: pH ideal=7, limit=8.5 · Turbidity ideal=0, limit=5 NTU · Conductivity ideal=0, limit=1500 · D.O. ideal=14, limit=5 · Temp ideal=25°C, limit=35°C
